    MiNyOnZ Family Rules
    1. No tile attacks
    2. Players holding heros can be rallied until released opposing can reinforce or rally back. Rally stops if hero is released.
    3. Players can zero one on one with no cores
    4. one on one hits acceptable if shields drop
    5. Wonder is a free for all. Participants are open for attack. No rules for wonder battles.
    6. No attacks on sh14 and lower (they can have their own battles). Sh15 and above can take and give hits.
